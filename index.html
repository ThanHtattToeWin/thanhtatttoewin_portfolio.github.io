Oxfordshire Housing and Council Tax Analysis

A data analytics project completed during my final year. I worked with publicly available datasets from the Office for National Statistics and local council websites, collecting information on housing prices and council tax rates across Oxfordshire, UK. I started by scraping, cleaning, and transforming the data using Python, then normalized it into Third Normal Form (3NF) and built a SQL database for efficient data management. After preparing the data, I used R to perform queries and create visualizations highlighting regional comparisons, tax band distributions, and notable trends.
